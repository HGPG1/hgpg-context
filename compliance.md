## SC House Bill 4754 (Act No. 204) - Team Names

- **Brokerage name disclosure:** Effective May 15, 2025 (already in force).
- **Team naming format:** `[Name] Team at [Brokerage]` - team names must end in "Team."
- **Compliance deadline:** May 15, 2027.

## Lando Law (SC closings)

- **Flat fee:** $550 total (seller rep + owner search)
  - Seller rep fee: $375
  - Owner search: $175 (max ~$225 in estate/probate cases)
- **How it's billed:** Out of seller proceeds, billed through buyer's attorney.
- **Why this policy:**
  - Actual seller representation at closing
  - Cost control vs unknown buyer attorney fees (have seen $800+)
  - Predictable line item
- **Logistics:** Brian rents office space at Lando Law, so coordination is easy.
