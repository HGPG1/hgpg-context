# Brand

## Colors (official brand guide)

| Name | Hex | Pantone |
|---|---|---|
| Dark navy | `#2A384C` | 7456C |
| Steel blue | `#A0B2C2` | 536C |
| Light steel | `#D1D9DF` | 5315C |
| Off-white | `#F0F0F0` | - |

**No green in brand.** Anything green is off-brand.

## Typography

- **Cooper Hewitt** (primary)
- **Sansita Regular** (secondary)
- **No Cormorant Garamond** - despite some legacy assets, this is not in the official brand guide.

## Voice rules

- **No em dashes** in any output. Use hyphens, commas, parentheses, or period breaks instead.
- No exclamation points in MLS descriptions.
- Present tense voice in listing copy.
- Never open MLS descriptions with a street address.
- No price in MLS description.
- Bolded headline at top of MLS description.
- Banned cliché list lives in the listing-wizard skill.

## Site design pattern

- Hero with headline + subhead + CTA
- Sticky nav: logo left, links center, CTA right
- Full-width sections
- Market stat cards: median price, DOM, active listings, mortgage rate
- Neighborhood-specific Charlotte content
- Timeline/step sections
- Testimonials with hard sale price + DOM numbers
